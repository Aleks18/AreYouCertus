About

Hi there, I'm glad you could join us.
Seriously. I mean it, REALLY seriously.

This website has been created to inform about the
good, bad and hidden ways in which our brain works.
Sometimes we are shocked at the abhorrent things
people do, sometimes surprised at how complex problems
we can solve and more often how easily it can be tricked.

We will try to cover all these things, and see what
triggers good behaviour from evil.
Hopefully you can join and discover more about yourself,
to see the world through more objective lens.
